Redesign: Content feature

Company: Quicksite guru

Role: UX/UI designer

Tool: Figma

Length: 2 months

Team: David,Rayyan,Meilani,May and Katharine

Completion Status: 85% complete

Year: 2024

Overview

Quicksite Guru is a platform designed to simplify website creation. It allows users to design and build websites without extensive coding knowledge. It typically provides features such as:

Pre-designed Templates: Users can choose from various customizable templates tailored for different industries and purposes.

AI-Powered Tools: Offers intelligent suggestions or automated customizations to help users create polished designs efficiently.

Content Features: Users can easily update and manage website content.

Beginner Coders:People learning to code who need a platform to practice and build websites.

Designers: Designers with some coding knowledge who want to bridge the gap between design and functionality.

Tech Enthusiasts:: People with a passion for coding who want to experiment with integrating their code into pre-built templates or platforms.

Coding and Customization Freedom: Quicksite Guru allows users to edit code, add custom features, and fully control their website’s design and functionality.

Content categorization: The platform categorizes content for easier management and quick adjustments, streamlining the process of updating and modifying website sections.

Affordable pricing: Get access to professional website-building tools at competitive prices, making it a cost-effective choice for both individuals and businesses.

I improved the UI within the Quicksite guru platform. My task was to redesign a content feature that allows users to add content to their website such as text, SVG, and asset features. However, I will only explain the text feature on this site.

Step1 Empathize: Understand user needs through feedback and observation.

Step2 Define: Identify key pain points and set improvement goals.

Step3 Test: Prototype and test design changes with users.

Step4 Final Outcome: Launch the improved text feature with enhanced usability.

Step5 Before and After comparison: Compare user experience and satisfaction before and after the redesign.

What is Content Feature?

Quicksite Guru is a platform that lets users create websites using AI technology and QSG templates. Users can customize content with the Fetch feature, including text, SVG, and assets. Additional content can also be added by placing the provided code in the instructions into the code feature. I will showcase the redesigned Text feature for this project.

Design Process

Step 1: Empathize

Quicksite Guru has a product feedback channel in our company to allow people to give feedback. I have decided to collect data in this area and interview developers, who are the main users, in order to understand their behavior and define the next steps.

Step 2: Define

I went through the feature step by step to find any issues and then shared my findings with the team.

Step 3: Test

I created high-fidelity wireframes and conducted usability tests with developers in our company to gain insights for product development.
