App Dashboard Design

Company: Quicksite guru

Role: UX/UI designer

Tool: Figma

Length: 2 weeks

Team: David,Rayyan,Meilani,May and Veronika

Completion Status: 55% complete

Year: 2024

Overview

Quicksite Guru is a platform designed to simplify website creation. It allows users to design and build websites without extensive coding knowledge. It typically provides features such as:

Pre-designed Templates: Users can choose from various customizable templates tailored for different industries and purposes.

AI-Powered Tools: Offers intelligent suggestions or automated customizations to help users create polished designs efficiently.

Content Features: Users can easily update and manage website content.

Beginner Coders:People learning to code who need a platform to practice and build websites.

Designers: Designers with some coding knowledge who want to bridge the gap between design and functionality.

Tech Enthusiasts:: People with a passion for coding who want to experiment with integrating their code into pre-built templates or platforms.

Coding and Customization Freedom: Quicksite Guru allows users to edit code, add custom features, and fully control their website’s design and functionality.

Content categorization: The platform categorizes content for easier management and quick adjustments, streamlining the process of updating and modifying website sections.

Affordable pricing: Get access to professional website-building tools at competitive prices, making it a cost-effective choice for both individuals and businesses.

Developed an advanced dashboard to provide comprehensive insights into website performance and visitor engagement metrics.

Step1 Benchmark: Compare with competitors or industry standards.

Step2 Ideate: Generate a wide range of ideas.

Step3 Feedback: Gather input from users or stakeholders to enhance the design.

Step4 Final Outcome: A high-fidelity representation of the final product.

Step5 Test: Assess how easily users can interact with the design.

Design Process

Step 1: Benchmark

Conducted research on competitors to gather insights and identify industry standards.

Step 2: Ideate

Created wireframes and explored various design ideas and interface options. These drafts were presented to the team for feedback and discussion, helping to refine the overall direction of the design.

Step 3: Feedback

Improved the designs by incorporating feedback from my team and analyzing user behavior within the application. Focused on minimizing content to enhance clarity and usability.

Step 4: Final Outcome

I implemented the design changes based on the feedback received and walked the team through the updated flow, ensuring everyone understands the improvements and how they enhance the user experience.

Step 5: Test

As the project is 55% complete, we have planned the scripts and tasks for the upcoming testing phase. We are preparing to conduct user testing soon to gather valuable feedback and make necessary adjustments before sending the design to the developer.
